Two Faces Grape

Ingredients:
1 cup red grapes , 1 cup green grapes , 1 cup grape juice ,1 tablespoon honey or simple syrup (adjust to taste) ,1 cup sparkling water ,Ice cubes ,Mint leaves for garnish
Instructions: Prepare the Grapes: Wash both red and green grapes thoroughly. Cut the grapes in half and remove the seeds if any. Blend the Grapes: In separate blender cups, blend the red grapes and green grapes separately until smooth. Strain (Optional): If you prefer a smoother mocktail, you can strain the grape purees to remove pulp. Use a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth. Mix with Grape Juice: In two separate bowls, mix each grape puree with 1/2 cup of grape juice. Sweeten: Add honey or simple syrup to each grape mixture, adjusting the sweetness according to your preference. Assemble: Fill half of the serving glass with the red grape mixture and the other half with the green grape mixture.
